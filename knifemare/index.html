Description

A nightmare hunter named Fray has set off to explore Reveille, the world of dreams, in hopes of uncovering the mystery behind the sudden surge of nightmares. These nightmares feed on the Rest Energy of ordinary people, as their subconscious forms drift aimlessly through Reveille while they sleep. Nightmare hunters have honed the ability to traverse Reveille through lucid dreaming and are the only people capable of fighting back against nightmares, but could this new threat be too much for Fray to handle?

Features

  • 3 stages, each with different enemies and bosses
  • Unique toss-and-catch gameplay
  • Highly customizable difficulty options
